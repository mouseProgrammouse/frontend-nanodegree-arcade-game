## Rules of the game

  In this game you have a Player and Enemies (Bugsüêû). The goal of the player is to reach the water, without colliding into any one of the enemies. Every time when player reached the water the score will increase and move back to the start position.

  The enemies move in varying speeds on the paved block portion of the scene. Once a the player collides with an enemy, the game is stop.

  Also player can collect üíégemsüíé to increase score.
